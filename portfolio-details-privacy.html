Overview

This tool helps non-technical users understand complex privacy policies by analyzing legal documents and extracting actionable insights on data collection, sharing, and rights.

Problem Statement

Most privacy policies are hard to interpret and contain dense legal language. Users often skip reading them and remain unaware of their rights and data usage implications.

Solution Approach

  • Fine-tuned a BERT-based model to classify policy sections by category (e.g., data use, retention, sharing).
  • Used spaCy and TextRank for named entity recognition and extractive summarization.
  • Built a Flask-based UI for document upload, processing, and result visualization.
  • Stored policy records in MongoDB with searchable tags and timestamps.
