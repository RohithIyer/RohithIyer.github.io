Privacy Policy Analyzer

Built an NLP-powered analyzer using spaCy and BERT to detect data collection and sharing practices in privacy policies.

🔧 Tools & Tech

  • Python
  • spaCy
  • Transformers
  • Flask
  • MongoDB

📈 What I Did

  • Designed an NLP pipeline to identify and classify clauses from policy texts.
  • Trained and fine-tuned BERT model for binary and multi-label classification.
  • Deployed via Flask app with search, highlight, and storage in MongoDB.
  • Achieved 94% accuracy in flagging risky clauses in lengthy policy documents.
